FAQ

Common Questions

Is UmpireDuty free to use?

Yes — creating an account, browsing umpires and making bookings is completely free. Umpires can list their services at no cost.

How do I know an umpire is qualified?

Every umpire profile shows their certification level (Amateur, Semi-Pro, Professional), their sports, and verified ratings from past bookings.

Can I book an umpire for multiple sports?

Absolutely. You can register for multiple sports and leagues. Each booking lets you choose which sport and league it's for.

How do I cancel or reschedule a booking?

You can cancel or edit any upcoming booking from your dashboard on the web portal or the mobile app. The umpire is notified automatically.

Is my club's data private?

Yes. Your booking details are only visible to you and the umpire involved. Admins can access aggregate data for platform management only.

Which countries are supported?

We currently support Australia, India, UK and more. New countries and regions are added regularly by our admin team.
